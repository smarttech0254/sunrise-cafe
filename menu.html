Sunrise Café Menu

Breakfast

Coffee

Coffee is a popular brewed drink made from roasted beans. It has a rich aroma, slightly bitter taste, and energizing effect due to caffeine.

Tea

Tea is a soothing beverage made by steeping dried leaves. It comes in many varieties such as black, green, and herbal blends.

Milk

Milk is a nutritious white liquid rich in calcium, protein, and vitamins.

Pancake

Pancakes are soft, fluffy flat cakes served with toppings like syrup, honey, or fruit.

Bread and Butter

Bread and butter is a classic breakfast choice, enjoyed plain or with extras like jam or honey.

Lunch

Grilled Chicken

Grilled chicken is marinated and cooked on a grill, served with rice, salad, or vegetables.

Sandwich

A sandwich is made with bread slices filled with meat, cheese, or vegetables.

Pasta

Pasta is boiled wheat dough served with sauces like tomato, cheese, or cream.

Beef Stew

Beef stew is slow-cooked meat with vegetables in a rich broth, served with rice or bread.

Supper

Fried Fish

Fried fish is coated in batter and deep-fried until crispy, served with chips or rice.

Rice and Chicken Curry

Chicken cooked in a spiced curry sauce served with rice.

Roasted Meat

Roasted meat is slow-cooked with spices until tender and juicy.

Mashed Potatoes

Mashed potatoes are boiled and mashed with butter and milk until creamy.
